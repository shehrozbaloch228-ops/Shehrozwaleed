Privacy Policy

Your privacy is a priority. This policy outlines our commitment to protecting it.

Last Updated: October 25, 2025

Welcome to the privacy policy for Shehroz Waleed. This policy details how I collect, use, protect, and handle your personally identifiable information (PII) when you visit my website (`the "Site"`) or engage with my services. Please read this policy carefully to get a clear understanding of my practices. By using this Site, you agree to the terms outlined below.

1. Data Controller

For the purpose of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other relevant data protection laws, the data controller is Shehroz Waleed, based in Lahore, Pakistan. You can contact me at the email address provided at the end of this policy.

2. Information I Collect

I collect information in two ways: information you provide to me directly and information collected automatically as you navigate the Site.

Personal Data You Provide

  • Contact and Communication Data: When you use my contact forms, subscribe to my newsletter, or communicate with me via email, phone, or WhatsApp, you may provide your name, email address, phone number, and the contents of your message.
  • Consultation Data: If you schedule a meeting using my Calendly link, I will collect the information required to facilitate that appointment, such as your name, email, and any notes you provide.

Usage Data Collected Automatically

  • Analytics and Log Data: My Site may use third-party services like Google Analytics to collect standard internet log information and details of visitor behavior patterns. This includes your IP address, browser type and version, device type, pages visited, time spent on pages, and other diagnostic data. This information is processed in a way that does not directly identify anyone.

3. How and Why I Use Your Information

The information I collect serves several key purposes:

  • To provide and maintain my services, including responding to your inquiries and scheduling consultations.
  • To manage and improve my Site by monitoring usage and analyzing trends.
  • To communicate with you, including sending newsletters or promotional materials if you have opted in. You can unsubscribe at any time.
  • To fulfill any legal or regulatory obligations.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Your privacy is paramount. I do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties.

I may share your data with trusted third-party service providers who perform functions on my behalf (e.g., website hosting, email services, analytics). These providers are contractually obligated to safeguard your data and are not permitted to use it for their own purposes.

I may also disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

5. Data Security

I am committed to protecting your data. I implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While I strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, I cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access – You have the right to request copies of your personal data.
  • The right to rectification – You have the right to request that I correct any information you believe is inaccurate or complete information you believe is incomplete.
  • The right to erasure – You have the right to request that I erase your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to object to processing – You have the right to object to my processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact me using the details below.

My Site contains links to external sites that are not operated by me (e.g., social media profiles, Calendly). If you click a third-party link, you will be directed to that site. I strongly advise you to review the privacy policy of every site you visit, as I have no control over their content or practices.
