The Path to Expertise

My journey has been defined by key milestones that shaped my unique, finance-first approach to e-commerce.

My story begins in a vibrant city where I learned the core values of hard work, community, and perseverance—principles that remain the bedrock of my professional ethic today.

Education

Academic Rigor in Accounting & Finance

At the University of Management and Technology (UMT), I earned my degree, which gave me a powerful lens to see beyond revenue and analyze the true financial health of a business. It's here I learned to read the story behind the numbers.

Specialization

I translated my financial knowledge into a specialized skill set for the Amazon ecosystem. I mastered industry-leading tools like Helium 10, Jungle Scout, and Keepa, not just for product research, but for comprehensive profitability analysis.

Partnership

Empowering U.S. Brands

Today, I apply this unique blend of skills as a freelance Amazon Growth Specialist, building data-driven strategies that empower U.S. brands to thrive. My journey allows me to bridge cultures and markets to deliver exceptional results.
