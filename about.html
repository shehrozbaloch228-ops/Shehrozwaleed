My Mission: Your Financial Success on Amazon

I am Shehroz Waleed, an Amazon Growth Specialist whose passion lies at the intersection of finance and e-commerce. My mission is to treat every client's business as my own, applying proven strategies and deep analysis to unlock sustainable, long-term growth on Amazon.

My commitment is to treat your business with the same analytical rigor and dedication as I would my own, ensuring every decision is a calculated step toward sustainable, long-term progress.

The Path to Expertise

My journey has been defined by key milestones that shaped my unique, finance-first approach to e-commerce.

Foundation

Roots in Turbat, Balochistan

My story begins in a vibrant city where I learned the core values of hard work, community, and perseverance—principles that remain the bedrock of my career.

Education

Academic Rigor in Accounting & Finance

At the University of Management and Technology (UMT), I earned my degree, which gave me a powerful lens to see beyond revenue and analyze the true drivers of business growth.

Specialization

Mastering the Amazon Marketplace

I translated my financial knowledge into a specialized skill set for the Amazon ecosystem. I mastered industry-leading tools like Helium 10, Jungle Scout, and SmartScout to drive results for my clients.

Partnership

Empowering U.S. Brands

Today, I apply this unique blend of skills as a freelance Amazon Growth Specialist, building data-driven strategies that empower U.S. brands to thrive globally.

My Guiding Principles

These three pillars are the foundation of every partnership and every strategy I build.

Financial Rigor

Every decision is viewed through a financial lens. I prioritize profit and sustainable ROI over vanity metrics, ensuring your growth is both impressive and intelligent.

Transparent Partnership

I believe in open, honest communication. You will always have a clear understanding of your account's performance, our strategy, and the 'why' behind every action we take.

Unwavering Integrity

Your brand is your most valuable asset. I am committed to protecting its reputation through ethical sourcing, strict MAP policy adherence, and a commitment to excellence in all operations.
